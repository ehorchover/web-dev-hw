The Batty Collection

Imagine each of these pictures below, are like chapters in an autobiography (this case the collection is the autobiography). Whether having a set plan or spontaneously going out to get pictures, the process to get those pictures from start to finish makes a story. There are so many elements that contribute to the story, some of the foundations are: setting, object(s), and time. For this set of photos below, these are all taken spontaneouly as I was aimlessly trying to learn about the town I recently moved to. As I was wandering around, I couldn't help but stop and observe the surroundings and take in the new sights, sounds, smells, ect. Whenever there was a

spot that particularly got my attention, I took a few pictures and carried on.

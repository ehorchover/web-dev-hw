How to Make: Orange Frosted Cinnamon Rolls

Will create an Instructable on how to make cinnamon rolls with frosting from a can.

You will need the following items:

  1. Baking sheet
  2. Non-stick Aluminon foil (optional)
  3. One can of store-baught Pillsbury Orange Rolls, or the type of your choice.

Follow these Steps

Proceed to Instructions
  • Turn the oven on to bake at 400degrees
  • Place aluminon foil on the baking sheet,
  • Space out Cinnamon rolls about an inch or two apart on the foil,
  • Place baking sheet in oven, when heated to 400degrees
  • Set the oven timer for 15-19 minutes, or until golden brown around edges.

    • Alomst Done...

    After the timer has gone off or the cinnamon rolls are golden brown, it is time to safely take them out of the oven.

    • Place the baking sheet on top of the stove or a cooling rack and wait for the rolls to cool.
    • While they are cooling,
      • get your frosting at your ready and either a plate or serving dish to put the finished product on.
    • Once the rolls have cooled off a little, ice each roll and place on your serving dish/plate.
    If there is any frosting left, top off any rolls or enjoy by itself. After a quick clean up, you are done and ready to enjoy some frosted Cinnamon rolls!

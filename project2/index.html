How to Make: Orange Frosted Cinnamon Rolls

This is an easy Instructable on how to make cinnamon rolls with frosting from a can.

You will need the following items:

  1. Baking sheet
  2. Non-stick Aluminon foil (optional)
  3. One can of store-baught Pillsbury Orange Rolls, or the type of your choice.
